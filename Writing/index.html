    Emily Dickinson

    Emily Dickinson has been such an inspiration to me when it comes to my writing. Everything she writes is so authentic and pure. She reminds me that even the smallest moments in life can hold great signifgance. Emily Dickinson work encourages me to write for myself as well and express emotions through my words.

    Maya Angelou

    Maya Angelou has had a profound influence on me. Her courage and wisdom can be seen in every line she writes. Her poetry is captivating to people all across the world using her writing to provoke positive changr, uplift, and empower. Maya Angelou has truly pushed me to write with both passion and purpose.
